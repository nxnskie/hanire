Vehicle Details

Toyota GR Supra

The Toyota GR Supra is a high-performance sports car featuring a turbocharged inline-six engine,

sharp handling, and modern styling, blending Toyota’s racing heritage with everyday drivability.

Dimensions Length: 4,379 mm
Width: 1,854 mm
Height: 1,294 mm
Wheelbase: 2,470 mm
Seating capacity: 2
Fuel tank capacity: 52 liters
Engine & Performance
Engine type: Twin-scroll turbocharged inline-6 (B58)
Displacement: 2,998 cc
Maximum power: 335 hp @ 5,000–6,500 rpm
Maximum torque: 500 Nm @ 1,600–4,500 rpm
Transmission: 8-speed automatic (6-speed manual available in some markets)
Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive (RWD)
Chassis & Suspension
Front suspension: MacPherson strut
Rear suspension: Multi-link independent
Front brakes: Ventilated disc, 4-piston fixed calipers (374 mm)
Rear brakes: Ventilated disc, 1-piston floating calipers (345 mm)
Wheels: 19-inch forged aluminum
Tires: Front – 255/35ZR19; Rear – 275/35ZR19
Interior & Features
8-way power-adjustable sports seats with memory
Dual-zone automatic climate control
Leather and Alcantara trim (varies by trim level)
LED headlamps, daytime running lights, and fog lamps
Performance & Dynamics
Near 50:50 front-to-rear weight distribution
Lightweight body construction for improved handling
